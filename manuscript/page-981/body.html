Chapter Xliv

Ease Not Peace

'A dull rotation, never at a stay,
 Yesterday's face twin image of to-day.'
          COWPER.

'Of what each one should be, he sees the form and rule,
 And till he reach to that, his joy can ne'er be full.'
          RUCKERT.
