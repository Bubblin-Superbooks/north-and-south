Chapter Xxix

A Ray Of Sunshine

'Some wishes crossed my mind and dimly cheered it,
 And one or two poor melancholy pleasures,
 Each in the pale unwarming light of hope,
 Silvering its flimsy wing, flew silent by—
 Moths in the moonbeam!'
         COLERIDGE.
