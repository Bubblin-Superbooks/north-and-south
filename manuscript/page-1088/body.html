Chapter Xlix

Breathing Tranquillity

'And down the sunny beach she paces slowly,
 With many doubtful pauses by the way;
 Grief hath an influence so hush'd and holy.'
          HOOD.

'Is not Margaret the heiress?' whispered Edith to her husband, as they were in their room alone at night after the sad journey to Oxford. She had pulled his tall head down, and stood upon tiptoe, and implored him not to be shocked, before she had ventured to ask this question.
