Chapter Xvii

What Is A Strike?

'There are briars besetting every path,
 Which call for patient care;
 There is a cross in every lot,
 And an earnest need for prayer.'
         ANON.

Margaret went out heavily and unwillingly enough. But the length of a street—yes, the air of a Milton Street—cheered her young blood before she reached her first turning. Her step grew lighter, her lip redder. She began to take notice, instead of having her thoughts turned so exclusively inward.
