Chapter Xlvii

Something Wanting

'Experience, like a pale musician, holds
 A dulcimer of patience in his hand;
 Whence harmonies we cannot understand,
 Of God's will in His worlds, the strain unfolds
 In sad, perplexed minors.'
          MRS. BROWNING.
